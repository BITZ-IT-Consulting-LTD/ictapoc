# Kenyatta National Hospital

**Sector:** Health

## 1. Executive Summary
Represents 'Health' cluster for balanced coverage; entity type: Agency. Included as Tier 3 for light‑touch desk review/survey.

## 2. Process Overview
**Objective:** Represents 'Health' cluster for balanced coverage; entity type: Agency. Included as Tier 3 for light‑touch desk review/survey.

## 3. As-Is Process Workflow
*Patient management involves registration, triage, and treatment. (Standard Hospital Workflow).*

| Step | Actor | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Patient | Patient registers at the reception desk and pays registration fee. |
| 2 | Nurse | Nurse conducts triage (vitals check) and records history. |
| 3 | Doctor | Doctor consults and prescribes treatment or diagnostic tests. |
| 4 | Patient | Patient pays for laboratory/pharmacy services. |
| 5 | Patient | Patient collects medication or undergoes procedures. |

## 4. Key Data Elements
### Inputs
- Patient Personal/Bio-data
- Insurance Card / NHIF Number
- Medical History Records
- Triage Vitals (BP, Temp, etc.)

### Outputs
- Patient File / EMR Record
- Diagnostic Lab Reports
- Prescription / Medication
- Discharge Summary

## 5. Pain Points & Opportunities
### Pain Points
- Loss of physical patient files.
- Long patient wait times at triage and pharmacy.
- Lack of interoperability between departments (Lab, Pharmacy, Billing).
- Revenue leakage in cash collections.

### Digitization Opportunities
- Comprehensive Electronic Medical Records (EMR).
- Telemedicine for remote consultations.
- AI-assisted diagnostics and radiology.
- Automated inventory management for pharmacy.
