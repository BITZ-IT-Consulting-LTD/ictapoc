# Kenya Government Services - Workshop Master Inventory & Architectural Mapping

## 1. Architecture: The 4 Layers of Interaction
Based on the **Kenya DSAP Architecture (Huduma Bridge)**, every government service is mapped across four distinct layers to ensure security, interoperability, and efficiency.

- **Layer 1: Access Channels (Citizen-Centric):** eCitizen Portal, Mobile Apps, USSD, and physical Huduma Centers.
- **Layer 2: Core Platform (Orchestration):** Workflow Engine (BPMN), Trust Hub (Identity via Maisha Namba), and NPKI for secure digital signatures.
- **Layer 3: Interoperability (Huduma Bridge):** Powered by KeSEL/X-Road for secure, peer-to-peer data exchange.
- **Layer 4: Foundational Registries & Payments:** Authoritative registries (IPRS, BRS, KRA) and the Government Payment Aggregator (GPA).
