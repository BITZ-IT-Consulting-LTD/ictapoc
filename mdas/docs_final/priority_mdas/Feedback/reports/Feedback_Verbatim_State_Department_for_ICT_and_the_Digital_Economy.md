### C1. Records Management: What feedback do you have about the records and document types identified as priorities?

### C2. Records Registry: What challenges related to records storage, classification, or registry work should be highlighted?
1. Inadequate storage space leading to congestion, overcrowded shelves, and poor arrangement of files
2. Poor classification systems causing misfiling, delays in retrieval, and inconsistent filing practices
3. Missing or lost files due to weak tracking and movement controls
4. Manual registry processes that are slow, labor-intensive, and prone to human error
5.Lack of standardized file indexing and referencing making it difficult to locate records quickly
6. Duplicate and redundant records which increase storage burden and confusion
7.Poor records security exposing sensitive records to unauthorized access, alteration, or loss
8. Fragmented file movement tracking where it is hard to know who has a file, where it is, and what action has been taken

### D1. Service Automation: What feedback do you have about the services selected for automation?
The services selected should be prioritized for automation to improve accessibility, efficiency, tracking, and accountability ensuring security, compliance, and proper process redesign.
