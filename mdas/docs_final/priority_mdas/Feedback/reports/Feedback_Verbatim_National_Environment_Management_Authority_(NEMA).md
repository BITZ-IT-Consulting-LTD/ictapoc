# Stakeholder Feedback Verbatim Report: National Environment Management Authority (NEMA)

> This report contains all verbatim feedback captured during the ICTA Validation Workshop for National Environment Management Authority (NEMA).

---
## Respondent: nan (Nema)

### Which workshop day is this feedback for?
Day 2: MDA Process Feedback

### Which breakout room are you in? (Day 2 only)
Room 3: Policy, Economy & Foundational Systems

### Participant MDA / Organization
Nema

### Which MDA presentation are you giving feedback on?
National Environment Management Authority (NEMA)

### A1. Current Processes: Looking at the process maps presented, what parts are accurate and what may need correction or clarification?
Nema provides different licenses, which have different process flows. The processes vary from one license to another. Only one process flow is captured. There is need to integrate all these processes and licenses with records management

### A2. Current Processes (Completeness): Are there services, steps, or activities that were not included in the process maps?
Yes, there are so many services that are left out, like registration and licensing of experts, environmental audits, effluent discharge licenses, emission licenses, and waste transport licenses.

### B1. Proposed Processes: What thoughts do you have about how the proposed future processes may work in practice?
the proposed changes are realistic provided they are intergrated with all environmental licences

### B2. Proposed Improvements: What comments do you have about the proposed quick wins and priority improvements?
I propose integration of all key services provided by the Authority

### C1. Records Management: What feedback do you have about the records and document types identified as priorities?
I have not seen process flow for records management,Only EIA is captured

### C2. Records Registry: What challenges related to records storage, classification, or registry work should be highlighted?
current registry practices are not reflected; there is need to integrate records management with all the processes.

### D1. Service Automation: What feedback do you have about the services selected for automation?
Automation will greatly improve service delivery, especially records management

### D2. Platform Integration: What considerations should be noted for integration with systems such as eCitizen, IPRS, or payment platforms?
Yes, integration with eCitizen is required for payments of licenses.

### E1. Policy and Regulations: What policy or legal issues should be considered for implementation?
Yes, there need to be a national digitization policy to guide the implementation of this

### E2. Institutional Arrangements: What comments do you have about the proposed governance structure, including the Digital Transformation Unit?
The proposal is feasible; policy development and more funds should be channelled to this course from the authority

### F2. Leadership and Ownership: What observations do you have about leadership support and institutional ownership?
The Authority has adequate and skilled staff; however, regular training is required on digitization and skill development on changing technology
